About The Project

The South Surra Project (RA-256) is one of the important infrastructure development project is taking place in the vicinity of Sheikh Jabber Al Ahmed Al Sabah Hospital. The project consists of construction and maintenance of Roads, Bridges, Troughs, Storm Water Detention Tank, Sewer and Other services for sections of South Surra area roadways in the vicinity of Jaber Al Ahmed Al Sabah hospital including segmental bridges of Sixth Ring Road and King Fahad Bin Abdul Aziz motorway (Road 40) in the vicinity.

The works will include upgrading the roads in the area and creating links to the major highways such as the 6th ring road and King Fahad Bin Abdul Aziz (Road 40) motorway. The work includes the diversion, upgrading, and protection of the old utilities connected to the upgraded road networks.

The total length of roadways to be improved is approximately 20 Kilometers.  Improvements include: modifications to the existing configurations of interchanges, addition of lanes, depressed roadways, grade separated intersections, improvement to signalized intersections and roundabouts. The project also includes preparing an inventory of existing utilities in the project areas and the preparation of diversion plans of these utilities taking into account future plans of service ministries.

The Project will involve modifications to Service Interchanges and System Interchanges configurations along the surrounding highways to provide free flow access to Jaber Al Ahmad Hospital and to the Ministry Zone. The internal roadways of South Surra Area will be a mix of depressed and elevated roadways with service roads. Bus routes and pedestrian bridges will be identified and service roads will be utilized for buses wherever feasible. Major utilities relocation is anticipated on this project.

Ministry of Public Works (MPW) has selected the consultant Parsons Brinckerhoff in association with Gulf Consult to upgrade the roads and accommodate for the extra flow of traffic leading to the governmental zone and upcoming Sheikh Jabber Al Ahmed Al Sabah Hospital in South Surra. Parsons Brinckerhoff & Gulf consult(JV) conducted traffic analysis and prepared pre-concept design, concept design, preliminary design and final detailed design to provide free flow access to Jaber Al Ahmad Hospital, from surrounding highways network and upgrading of internal roadway network of South Surra Area as required.

SCOPE OF WORKS

  1. At grade Roads and Bridges
    1. 2 Km of 11 lane Road (6 Lane + 5Lane)
    2. 5 Km of 8 lane Road (4 Lane +4Lane) & associated CD roads and service roads.
    3. 3 Km of 6 lane Road (3 Lane at each directions)
    4. 1 Over trough vehicular bridges (Post tensioned concrete deck box girders).
    5. 5 Over trough Vehicular slab bridges (Reinforced concrete).
    6. 4 Over trough Utility bridges (Reinforced concrete deck U Girders).
  2. Depressed Roads (Troughs)
    1. 700m – 3 Lane road (1 reinforced concrete trough & associated tension piles)
    2. 800m – Single lane (1 reinforced concrete trough & 2 reinforced concrete trough with associated tension piles)
  3. Interchange & Elevated Bridges:
    1. 4 Interchanges that include (8) elevated vehicular bridges (Post tensioned precast segmental concrete box girders deck flyovers)
    2. 2.4 Km associated reinforced earth ramps/ mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) retaining walls.
  4. Reinforced Concrete Retaining walls
  5. Storm water Drainage for roadways, troughs and elevated vehicular bridge decks including associated reinforced concrete box culverts and underground detention tank.
  6. Utilities
    1. Relocation/reconstruction, diversion & protection of existing services and utilities
      1. Sanitary & water pipelines
      2. Pipelines by micro tunneling
      3. Street lighting
      4. HT Electrical power lines
      5. 11KV Electrical power lines and LT electrical cables
    2. RC Utility box culvert
    3. Civil works (PTT) services
  7. Road markings & signs, Traffic Signaling and control, area traffic control (ATC) devices and associated works.
  8. Two (2 Nos) of Pedestrian bridges
  9. Eight (8 Nos) Bus stop passenger shelters
  10. Hard landscaping (footpath tiling etc), civil works for irrigation pipelines associated with soft landscaping.
  11. Maintenance and protection of traffic during construction (temporary detours)
  12. Environmental protection procedure.
  13. Demolition and removal of two vehicular bridges and one pedestrian bridges.
  14. Other related works for local roads and junctions as per contract.

PROJECT OBJECTIVES

  1. Providing the signal free access to Jaber Al Ahmad Hospital
  2. Minimizing the traffic congestions in the vicinity of Jaber Hospital and ministry area
  3. Meet future traffic demands.
  4. Increasing the capacity of roads
  5. Improving and modernizing the existing utilities network
  6. Improve road facilities and services
  7. Improving road safety standards

PROJECT ZONES

For work easiness; the total project is divided into five (5) zones which are named as Zone 1, Zone 2, Zone 3, Zone 4 and Zone 5.

Zone 1 Trough M13, SWD Tank & Culvert along Street 401
Zone 2 I/C-52B Bridges on section of 6th Ring Road
Zone 3 I/C-52A Bridges on section of 6th Ring Road
Zone 4 I/C-44 Bridges on section of King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Road (40)
Zone 5 Trough M-14 & M 34 along the Ebrahim Bin Abdulla Al Muzain Street (404)

INTERCHANGE 44

Interchange I/C-44 with three bridges with pile foundation and pre casting & erection of segmental girders.

  • Interchange44-Bridge 1: Bridge Connecting Road404 in Hateen area and Road206 in Mubarak Al Abdullah side over King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Motorway.
  • Interchange44- Bridge 2: To reach the Kuwait City bound lanes of King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Motorway from Mubarak al Abdullah Bound lanes of Road 404 in Hateen Area.
  • Interchange44-Bridge 3: To reach the Hateen area bound lanes of Road 404 from Kuwait City bound Lanes of King Fahd Bib Abdul Aziz Motorway.

INTERCHANGE 52A

Interchange 52A with three elevated vehicular bridges with pile foundation and Post tensioned precast segmental concrete box girders deck flyovers associated mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) retaining walls.

  • Interchange 52A - Bridge 1: To reach the service road of Sixth Ring Motorway leading to the free right entry of Governmental zone from the Jahra bound lanes of Sixth Ring Motorway over the free right turn from King Fahd Bin Abdul Aziz Motorway to Sixth Ring Motorway.
  • Interchange 52A - Bridge 2: To take the free right turn of Road 400 in Governmental area to Sixth Ring Motorway over the Service Road of Sixth Ring Road.
  • Interchange 52A - Bridge 3: To enter Road 400 in Governmental area from the Messila bound lanes of Sixth Ring Motorway over the Sixth Ring Motorway.

INTERCHANGE 52B

Three elevated vehicular bridges with pile foundation and Post tensioned precast segmental concrete box girders deck flyovers associated mechanically stabilized earth (MSE) retaining walls.

  • Interchange 52B - Bridge 1: To reach Sheikh Al Jaber Al Sabah Hospital bound lanes of Road 401 in Governmental area from Messila bound lanes of Sixth Ring Motorway over the Sixth Ring Motorway.
  • Interchange 52B – Bridge 2: To reach the Messila bound lanes of Sixth Ring Motorway from the Sabhan bound lanes of Road 401 in Governmental area over Sixth Ring Motorway.
  • Interchange 52B – Bridge 3: To reach Sheikh Al Jaber Al Sabah Hospital bound lanes of Road 401 in Governmental area from Jahra bound lanes of Sixth Ring Motorway.

Trough M13

Trough M13 consist of 500m depressed road. This trough is helpful for those vehicles getting out from the Sheikh Al Jaber Al Sabah Hospital on street 401 and providing a free U- turn to street 401 vehicles.

Trough M14 & M34

Trough M14 & M34 consist of 800m depressed road, Trough M14 consist of Two troughs,Three vehicle Bridges and one utility bridge, Trough at M34 consist of two vehicular bridges and one utility bridge.This trough is to reach directly to Sheikh Al Jaber Al Sabah Hospital & Zahra areal by avoiding the existing signal at the junction of street 400 and street 404.
